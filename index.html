SHAKING INCUBATOR


The incubator shaker is a temperature controlled biochemical instrument combining incubation and shaking functions. It is used in cell culture, fermentation, hybridization, biochemistry and research of enzyme and cell tissues which require a high quality of temperature control and shaking speed.

  • Shaking incubators have a specialized design and you can use this equipment to incubate and agitate or shake samples simultaneously. They're suitable for different applications in laboratories. This equipment is for cell aeration and culture, experiments on solubility, and more.

  • With this equipment, you don't have to place a separate shaker within your incubator.   • The shaking incubator is an indoor instrument that should only be used in an area with low temperature, little dust, and good air circulation.
